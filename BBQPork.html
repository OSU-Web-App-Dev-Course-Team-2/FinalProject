BBQ Pork for sandwiches
  • 1 (14 ounce) can beef broth
  • 3 pounds boneless pork ribs
  • 1 (18 ounce) bottle barbeque sauce
  1. our can of beef broth into slow cooker, and add boneless pork ribs.
  2. Cook on High heat for 4 hours, or until meat shreds easily.
  3. Remove meat, and shred with two forks.
  4. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  5. Transfer the shredded pork to a Dutch oven or iron skillet, and stir in barbeque sauce.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until heated through.
