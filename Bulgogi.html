Bulgogi
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds/li>
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound beef top sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1/2 yellow onion, chopped
  1. In a large resealable plastic bag, combine soy sauce, sesame oil, sesame seeds, garlic, sugar, salt, black pepper.
  2. Place beef, carrots, and onions in the bag; seal, and shake to coat the vegetables and beef with the sauce.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 2 1/2 hours. I prefer to marinate mine overnight.
  4. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
  5. Remove meat and vegetables from marinade, and place on a large sheet of aluminum foil; seal. Discard marinade.
  6. Place on grill, and cook for 15 to 20 minutes, or to desired doneness.
