Best Chicken Ever
  • 16 Ounce Package Penne Pasta
  • 2 Tablespoons Butter
  • 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • 4 Skinless, Boneless Chicken Breast Halves
  • - Cut into thin Strips
  • 2 Clove Garlic, Minced
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste
  • 1 1/4 Cups Heavy Cream
  • 1/4 Cup Pesto
  • 3 Tablespoons Grated Parmesan Cheese
  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until aldente; drain;
  2. Heat butter and olive in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Saute chichken and garlic until chicken is almost cooked.
  4. Reduce heat and stir in salt, pepper, cream, pesto and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Cook until chicken is no longer pink inside.
  6. Stir in cooked pasta.
